Shipping and return policy

SHIPPING POLICY

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates

Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout.

  • Standard (US) - 3–7 business days - $5.99 or Free over $50
  • Expedited (US) - 2–3 business days - $12.99
  • International - 7–21 business days - Calculated at checkout

International Shipping

We ship worldwide! Please note that your order may be subject to import duties and taxes (including VAT), which are incurred once a shipment reaches your destination country. We are not responsible for these charges if they are applied.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. Please allow 24–48 hours for tracking information to become available.

RETURN & EXCHANGE POLICY

30-Day Return Window

We want you to love your purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, you may return eligible items within **30 days** of delivery for a refund or exchange.

Eligibility for Returns

  • Item must be unused, in the same condition that you received it.
  • Must be in the original packaging.
  • Proof of purchase is required.

Non-returnable Items

  • Gift cards
  • Clearance or final sale items
  • Personalized or custom products

How to Start a Return

Email us at **returns@\[yourdomain].com** with your order number and the reason for return. We will send you a return shipping label and instructions on how and where to send your package.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we’ll notify you if the refund was approved. If approved, you’ll be automatically refunded on your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? Let us know in your return request email and we’ll help arrange an exchange, subject to availability.

If you have any questions, please contact our support team at support@rainydays.com.
